In 1993, with Bollywood actor Dharmendra as brand ambassador, the company introduced the tagline of "Khoob Jamega Rang Jab Mil Bhaithenge Teen Yaar. Aap, Mein Aur Bagpiper", and the brand continues to use the tagline in its branding. Bagpiper had many Bollywood celebrities, along with Ashok Kumar, Dharmendra, Sunny Deol, Jackie Shroff, Shahrukh Khan and Ajay Devgun, as marketing ambassadors. Since 2005 to 2010, Bagpiper was Country's biggest marketed whisky brand, when OC (Officer's Choice), which is made by Allied Blenders & Distillers (ABD), overtooked it.
